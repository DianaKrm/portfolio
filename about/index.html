Selected Work

BloomBox - Branding

Brand Identity • Logo Design • Mockups

Role

Designer

Tools

Illustrator • Photoshop • Figma

Overview

BloomBox is a floral subscription concept focused on monthly flower deliveries and eco-friendly packaging. The goal was to create a soft, modern identity that feels elegant, approachable, and gift-worthy.

Goal

Develop a cohesive brand identity that reflects sustainability and warmth while remaining visually refined and simple.

Process

I began by defining the brand direction, focusing on sustainability and recyclable packaging. From there, I explored logo concepts and visual directions using soft pastel tones to reflect the floral theme. After testing multiple variations, I refined the logo into a simplified letter-based mark for clarity and versatility. The identity was then applied across packaging and print mockups to demonstrate consistency.

Final Result

The final deliverables include a logo system, brand color palette, and realistic mockups such as business cards, packaging, and a tote bag to showcase real-world application.

Locora - App Concept

UI/UX Design • App Design • Prototyping

Role

UI/UX Designer

Tools

Figma

Overview

Locora is a location-based app concept designed to help users discover trending places based on their preferences.

Goal

Design a clean and intuitive mobile interface that allows users to explore nearby places through personalized recommendations and map previews.

Process

I began by identifying the core functionality and outlining the user flow. After defining the structure, I created wireframes and refined them into high-fidelity UI screens in Figma. The design process involved multiple refinements to balance clarity and visual appeal. I developed an interactive prototype to simulate user interaction and navigation.

Final Result

The final concept includes structured navigation, personalized recommendation sections, and an interactive prototype demonstrating the app experience.

Japanese Food Ordering Website

Web Design • Front-End Development

Role

Designer & Front-End Developer

Tools

Figma • HTML • CSS • JavaScript • VS Code

Overview

A restaurant-focused website concept designed to provide a clean browsing and ordering experience for Japanese cuisine.

Goal

Create a responsive and visually structured website that allows users to browse menu items and navigate smoothly toward checkout.

Process

I first developed the layout concept in Figma to establish structure and visual hierarchy. Rather than fully finalizing the design there, I transitioned into development early and built the interface using HTML and CSS. During development, I refined layout spacing, alignment, and responsiveness directly in code, focusing on clarity and clean structure.

Final Result

A fully coded website with structured navigation, responsive layout, and clear menu presentation.

The Ordinary - Social Media Concept

Visual Design • Illustration

Role

Designer

Tools

Procreate • Photoshop • Figma • Illustrator

Overview

A conceptual social media content series created to present skincare products in a more stylized and distinctive way.

Goal

Create visually engaging social media content that stands out while maintaining brand alignment.

Process

Instead of relying only on product imagery, I created custom illustrated elements in Procreate to develop a more original visual direction. These illustrations were combined with PNG product assets to build cohesive layouts. The focus was on balance - blending minimal branding with creative visual storytelling.

Final Result

A series of stylized social media posts combining illustration and product imagery for a cohesive visual concept.
